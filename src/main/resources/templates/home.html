Common Questions & Answers

Creating a new group is simple. Navigate to the "Groups" section, click on "Create Group," and fill out the required information such as the group name and description. Once you've completed these steps, your group will be live and ready for members to join.

You can browse through various groups by visiting the "Groups" page. Once you find a group that suits you, simply click "View Group" and then "Join Group" to become a member.

Group announcements will appear at the towards the bottom of the group’s page. In the future, you'll receive notifications via email if you're subscribed to announcement updates.

You can view upcoming events by visiting the "Events" page, where events are listed in chronological order.

To create an event, go to the "Events" page and click on "Create Event." Fill in the event details such as title, date, time, location, and any other relevant information. You can also choose to make it a single-day or multi-day event. After saving, your event will be visible to the community.

Yes, you can update or cancel an event at any time. Go to the event page and click "Edit Event".
